PavoMe - multimedia client for Twitter

What is PavoMe?
It's a Twitter client for mobile phones that support Java.
Will it work on my Phone?
It should work on most phones that support J2ME, like Nokia and SonyEricsson phones.
Multimedia?
You can easily upload photos, videos and even audio recordings from PavoMe. See some photos taken by our users.
How to get it?
Justfor download instructions. PavoMe uses OAuth so we won't ask for your Twitter username and password.
PavoMe is beta software. Icons used on the website and in the application come from (or based on) Albook extended icon set.
